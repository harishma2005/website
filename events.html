ECE Association (ECEA)

The biggest asset of the department is the presence of the ECE Association (ECEA) which works round the clock to make the department bigger and better.
ECEA functions mainly as a student organisation comprising around 1000 student members and headed by HOD.
ECEA's prime activities include staging RESONANCE, an intra-college technical event and VISION, an inter-college technical symposium and other activities include conducting various workshops and seminars through which students sculpt their young engineersâ€™ mind according to their will.

VISION & RESONANCE

VISION is a prestigious national level inter-college technical symposium conducted every year in March.
It provides a national platform for students to showcase their talents in hardware, software and paper presentation competitions, displaying cutting edge technology and research.
Also, it motivates students through workshops conducted by industrial experts and keynote speakers from academia.
It provides ample opportunities for the multi-pronged growth of the student fraternity. The students are given an opportunity to unleash the technical geeks through the intra-college technical event RESONANCE. The students exhibit their technical expertise through project displays, paper presentations, robotics events and several on the spot events.

Biomedical Engineer's Association - Bio Spectra & Medevolv

Bio spectra, an annual inter college technical symposium has reached heights year after year.
With its inception in 2012, the symposium has had a progressive journey till now.
Having workshops with innovative topics, medical expo and symposium have advanced from start of Bio Spectra onwards.
Students from all over India participate in the various enthralling events, workshops and guest lecture.
Medevolv, is the biomedians biggest ever intra college symposium with new spirit and innovations .
Students organize various events and paper presentation and workshops to develop their skills.

IEEE Students Chapter

Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers is an international community devoted to recognizining and encouraging technical innovations.
Being its member gives one the opportunity to attend various lectures conducted by IEEE and present various papers under the IEEE banner.
As a part of ECE Association activities a student chapter has been opened that has 30 student members and 2 staff members.
ECE Association gives concession to join as a member in IEEE so as to encourage patronage.
